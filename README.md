
# Sales Performance & Profitability Analysis (Excel)

## Business Question
Why did sales performance decline during certain periods, and is the issue driven by demand or profitability?

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Charts
- KPI calculations

## Key KPIs Analyzed
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Overall Margin
- Units Sold

## Analysis Summary
- Identified Q3 as the weakest quarter in terms of revenue, profit, and margin.
- Found that margin decline, not demand drop, was the primary driver of profit risk.
- Prioritized the Electronics category due to high revenue but relatively lower margin, indicating pricing or cost optimization opportunities.
